Video editing & Videography

Video editing is the process of arranging, modifying, and refining video footage to create a polished final product. It involves cutting out unnecessary parts, sequencing clips, adding transitions, color grading, sound design, and integrating visual effects to enhance the storytelling or visual appeal. Editors often use software like Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, DaVinci Resolve, or specialized VFX and CGI tools for compositing, like After Effects or Blender.

Graphic Design

Graphic design is a visual art that involves transforming ideas and concepts into beautiful and effective images and illustrations for communication. It aims to convey specific messages in an attractive and impactful way using colors, typography, images, and lines. Graphic designers work on creating various designs such as logos, posters, advertisements, print materials, and digital content.

3D Modeling & CGI Videos

CGI, short for Computer-Generated Imagery, refers to images generated by computers. It encompasses any images, animations, or visual effects produced using computer software and is commonly used in movies, television, games, advertising, as well as in architecture and virtual reality. This technique involves creating scenes, objects, and details that might be difficult or costly to capture in real life.

VFX compositing

VFX Compositing is the process of combining multiple visual elements from different sources into a single, cohesive scene, often used in film, television, and digital media. The goal is to make these elements look as though they were captured together at the same time and in the same place, even if they were filmed separately.
